MODEL v1 BREAKDOWN
Factor Weights
Diamond Oracle v1 is a 14-factor baseball prediction model built from the ground up for the 2026 season. The model weights xFIP — expected fielding-independent pitching — as the single most predictive starter metric (outperforming ERA and FIP), stripping out defense and luck to isolate pitcher quality. Bullpen ERA carries substantial weight because late-inning leverage situations in baseball are disproportionately decisive. The model separates platoon advantage (SP handedness vs lineup), rest days, park factor (separate HR and run-scoring adjustments), and recent L10 form weighted at 70% of trajectory value.
★ Title Contender Profile

World Series champions almost universally have: team ERA below 3.80, bullpen ERA below 3.60, team wRC+ above 108, run differential above +80 over a full season, and win at least 55% on the road. Model teams matching all criteria: Dodgers, Phillies, Braves, Astros, Mariners. Highest World Series probability in simulation: Dodgers (18.4%), Phillies (11.2%), Braves (10.8%).
